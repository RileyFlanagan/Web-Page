Colosseum Info/History

The Colosseum is one of Rome's most recognizable classic buildings. It was the first permanent amphitheatre to be raised in Rome and also with the the most impressive arena the world has ever seen.

Why come?

The Colosseum is definitely one of the most historical and interesting buildings throughout all of Rome. The arena is huge and is such an amazing sight to see. Everyone knows it's a "must see" for all first time visitors in Rome. Being able to actually see where the fights took place and the huge seating area is breathtaking.

Other things you can do and see while visiting Rome:

  • Go to the vatican museum to see influential art from the Renaissance Period
  • Go to the Villa Borghese Gardens to see the beautiful garden for a relaxing sight
  • If you love to shop the Via Condotti is for sure the place to be!
